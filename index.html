Elementrix is a card game designed to turn the confusing and complicated world of chemistry into a strategic battle of wits. Instead of just memorizing the periodic table from a textbook, players use a deck of cards to actually build chemical compounds and trigger reactions.

Download Rules

Why Elementrix?

Students are first introduced to the concept of atoms and how they stick together. The game simplifies valency, being a perfect match as well for the pre-algebra skills typically mastered in 7th or 8th grade.

Strategic Play

The game features three main types of cards: Element cards representing different atoms, and reaction cards that change the environment (like adding heat or a catalyst).
